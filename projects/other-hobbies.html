Ring Alarm

I met a phenomenal woman at an Israeli dance camp who had an interesting problem. She couldn't wear a watch, and she wanted a timer she could use in a SCIF, so she could better keep track of time. She normally wears a cute little golden ring watch, but watches of this size don't have alarms, and generally have limited interfaces. I'm not an electrical engineer, but I did some breadboarding back in high school, and I've done a bit of research. I stumbled upon a cheap digital ring watch by a Japanese company called Pikoo I ordered it in the mail, took it apart, and reasoned that I could probably create a similar sized ring alarm if I substituted the circuit board for one that carried the program I wanted. The ring is roughly 15mmx15mmx6mm, and I can find a bunch of parts that are barely at this level of miniturization. I'm currently ordering them off the web, and I'll see about breadboarding something together.
