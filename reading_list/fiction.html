Foundation by Isaac Asimov

Foundation is bizaare to read in 2022. Everything is so simply parallel to the 1940s world that Asimov existed in that many of the tropes of future technology simply don't exist yet. Everything is "nuclear butter knives" and "nuclear washing machines," and so the bare metaphors of what Asimov is tell the reader are incredibly accessible. The idea of a civilization that could transcend the stars but not produce nuclear fuel is utterly mindmelting in our current star-trek informed notion of science fiction.
